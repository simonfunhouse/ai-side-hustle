How To Use It

You're not buying messages to copy blindly.

The original emails and posts are included because they show what a genuine campaign looked like — their value is in being real, not polished. Your product, audience, prices, bonuses and launch date will all be different. Keep the strategy behind each message; change everything else:

Rewrite Before You Send
  • Product name and description
  • Prices and commission structure
  • Launch date and affiliate link
  • Bonuses, deliverables and approval wording
  • The personal story — this one's mine

The goal: messages that sound like you — not like Simon From Scotland promoting the AI Side Hustle Starter Kit.

Worth Knowing

Don't approve every affiliate who applies.

It's tempting to approve everyone the moment applications start coming in — you're grateful for someone's interest. But approval is a business decision, not a reflex. The Companion gives you safer wording to start from:

"Mention [KEYWORD] in the notes box so I can identify your application. All applications are reviewed individually."
— Approval reply, adapted from the original launch

The original approval wording from my own launch is included too, alongside editor's notes on why you'll want to adjust it to match your own policy.
